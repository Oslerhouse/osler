Osler House

The home of clinical students in Oxford.
Learn more






What?

Osler House (named after Sir William Osler) is a JCR exclusively for clinical medical students.

Why?

Whether you enjoy sports, bops, societies, or just a social space to hang out with your friends, Osler House has something to offer to everyone.

Where?

The building itself is the social hub of the Clinical School, be sure to head over to enjoy a subsidised lunch and bop the night away on Fridays after a long week in the hospital.
